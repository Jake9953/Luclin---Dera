About Us

Luclin Enterprise Limited is a dynamic and versatile company registered in the Republic of Kenya under the Company Act Cap. 486. The company was incorporated in the year 2023. We specialize in delivering top-notch engineering, construction, material supply, and general supplies. Our company has established a reputation for delivering high-quality, innovative, and cost-effective solutions to meet our clients' needs.

Vision

To be the leading engineering and construction firm, recognized for our innovative solutions, high standards of safety, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Mission

To provide our clients with the highest quality engineering and construction services, through a blend of expertise, innovation, and commitment to excellence, while fostering a culture of safety, integrity, and continuous improvement.
